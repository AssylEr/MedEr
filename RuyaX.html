How to Get Started

Joining the RuyaX community is simple. Follow these easy steps to begin your journey:

  • Step 1: Sign Up Securely
    Create an account in seconds using your email or Google account. We prioritize your security from the very first step.
  • Step 2: Discover & Explore
    Swipe through a personalized, full-screen feed of content. The more you watch and interact, the better our recommendations become. Use the search feature to find new creators and trends.
  • Step 3: Create & Share
    Tap the upload button to share from your gallery, or use our in-app camera to capture a new moment. Add a title and description, and share it with the world!

Your Privacy, Your Control

We believe in being upfront and clear about how we handle your data. Your trust is our top priority.

  • You Are in Control: We only require your email for account creation. You decide what other information to share on your profile, with privacy settings to control the visibility of your age, gender, and country.
  • A Safe Community: We provide tools to ensure your safety. Block users you don't want to interact with and report any content that violates our guidelines.
  • Data Deletion: You have the right to request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data directly within the app.

This is a summary. For complete details, we encourage you to read our full Privacy Policy.
